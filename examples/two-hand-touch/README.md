# Two-Hand Touch — Breaststroke

Frame samples illustrating the two-hand touch required at turns and finishes in competitive breaststroke. Per FINA/USA Swimming rules, both hands must contact the wall simultaneously and at the same level — staggered touches, single-hand touches, and submerged touches are all DQ-able faults.

## Frames

Five frames captured chronologically from a swim video, showing the moment of wall contact across one or more turns/finishes.

`frame_01.png` through `frame_05.png` — ordered by timestamp.

## Purpose

Reference set for the swim-analysis skill. Helps the analyzer evaluate whether a swimmer's wall touch satisfies the simultaneous two-hand requirement when reviewing competitive breaststroke footage.
